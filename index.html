About

I'm Evelina

I am Evelina Markaj, born on January 22, 2001 in Peja. I completed primary school in 'Asdren', high school in 'Bedri Pejani' gymnasium, while I attended university studies from 2019-2022 at AAB College in the field of Painting and Visual Arts in Fushё Kosovё-Prishtinё where I got my bachelor degree.I took part in exhibitions held in primary school. Also during my university studies, such as: participation in the book "Vepra e saj" by Alketa Xhafa, where I made a portrait of one of the pioneers of Kosovo, pharmacist Mediha Broqi-Beqiri, exhibition for the day of November 28 with the Youth Assembly of Kosovo in Prishtinё. I aspire to do much more in the near future with more personal and collective exhibitions.
